Campus Hulchal

 


  • HINDI DEBATE COMPETITION        (Meghraj SuranaVyakhyan Mala) :- Hindi debate competition is regularly being organized in the college yearly in memories of ‘Late Sh.MeghrajSurana’, to enhance the knowledge of students in Indian culture & Tradition .
 
  • DHARA DARSHAN GEOGRAPHYEXHIBITION: – Exhibition is the best way to display one’s ideas. DHARA DARSHAN is organized in the college campus in which students made various charts, models and projects related to the subject matter like dispensing of volcanoes, global warming and so on.
   
  • SEMINARS & GUEST LECTURERS: – To cope up with the changing scenario seminars and guest lecturer plays a vital role in imparting knowledge. College regularly organizes various seminars and guest lecturers for the overall development of the students.
 
  • ACADEMIC TOURS: -Thecollege not only imparts education through the medium of books but also through educational tours which enhance the knowledge of the students regarding different historical & other places of great value and importance.
 
  • REFLECTION :-To create asense of belongingness and make them feel comfortable with the new environment of the college an yearly ‘welcome party” and ‘Thanks party’ are organized regularly.
    1. NUPUR :– College celebrate yearly the annual Function and Prize Distribution Ceremony named ‘NUPUR’ in the Auditorium. The function includes various Cultural Programmes like :-
    • RANGOLI COMPETITION
    • MEHANDI COMPETITION
    • DANCE COMPETITION
    • SONG COMPETITION
    • INTER CLASS SPORTS COMPETITION
  • and presentation of prizes, Mementos and certificates to the students, who excelled in academics, debates, co-curricular activities, sports and Inter college competitions.
